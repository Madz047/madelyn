I build finance systems with clear, repeatable steps so the work survives turnover. Outcome: consistency and accuracy that don't rely on a single individual.

I design reconciliation systems that flag discrepancies between records and reality. Outcome: accurate stock levels and earlier, smarter reordering.

Reporting takes hours of manual work.

I build dashboards that pull the numbers together automatically. Outcome: instant visibility instead of end-of-month scrambles.

I document workflows and write SOPs people actually follow. Outcome: knowledge that's captured, shareable, and trainable.
